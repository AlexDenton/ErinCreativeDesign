Erin Fonzi Denton | Creative Designer

hello!

My name is Erin Fonzi Denton

and I am a graphic designer and artist
in Orlando, Florida. I love learning
new things. Whether its that awesome
techno thingy they just annouced at a
conference, or a 300 year old printing
press you found in your basement, both
sound like party time to me.

I graduated in May 2011

from University of Central Florida with
a B.F.A. in Fine Art with specialization
in graphic design. At UCF I was also
an officer for the Graphic Design
Student Association. One day, I would
like to continue my education and go
to grad school for a M.F.A.

My career goals

are to work creativly in an academic
institution. My interests are in design,
teaching, and using digital media to
educate. I also enjoy doing freelance
and art commisions on the side, so
what ever you need I am game. If you
have a jukebox, I'll hit it.
